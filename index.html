1. Overview

GPT Hub is a Chrome extension designed to help you organize and manage your custom ChatGPT GPTs. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our approach to data handling, which can be summarized simply: your data never leaves your device.

2. Information We Store Locally

The extension stores the following information using Chrome's local storage API:

  • GPT Information: Names, descriptions, and URLs of GPTs you add
  • Organization Data: Teams and categories you create
  • User Preferences: Theme settings (light/dark mode), view preferences
  • Usage Statistics: Last used timestamps and use counts (stored locally only)

Important: All this data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We have no access to this information.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Analytics or usage telemetry
  • Any data stored in the extension
  • ChatGPT account information
  • Conversation contents or AI interactions

4. How the Extension Works

Website Content Access: The extension reads GPT names and descriptions from ChatGPT pages when you visit them. This is done solely to enable the auto-detection feature that helps you quickly add GPTs to your hub. This information is processed locally and stored only on your device.

Host Permissions: The extension requires permission to access chatgpt.com and chat.openai.com domains only to detect when you're viewing a custom GPT page. No other websites are accessed.

Since all data remains on your device and we never transmit or receive any information, your data is as secure as your Chrome browser installation. We cannot access, modify, or delete your data - only you can.

5. Third-Party Services

GPT Hub does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or external servers. The extension operates entirely locally on your device.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. Your information never leaves your device and is never accessible to us or any third parties.

7. Data Export and Deletion

Export: You can export your GPT collection and teams using the built-in export feature. This creates a file that you control.

Deletion: To delete all extension data, simply uninstall the extension from Chrome. This will remove all locally stored information.

8. Children's Privacy

GPT Hub does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13, as we do not collect any information at all.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Since we don't collect user data, we cannot notify you directly of changes, so please review this policy periodically.

10. Open Source

GPT Hub is committed to transparency. You can review our extension's code to verify our privacy practices. We believe in user control and data ownership.
